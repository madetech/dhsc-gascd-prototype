Get adult social care data: sign in or request access

Find out how to access this digital insight tool for adult social care data in England.

About this service

Get adult social care data is a digital insight tool that provides access to a wide range of datasets about adult social care in England.

The service brings together all relevant datasets from the NHS, Care Quality Commission (CQC), Skills for Care, care providers and other data sources.

You can use this service to:

  • identify and monitor data in areas including demand, capacity, service quality, care outcomes, workforce, and market sustainability
  • explore and visualise data with interactive tools that help identify patterns, trends and insights
  • support decision-making and strategic planning by downloading datasets and charts in formats you can integrate into your own analyses

Who can use the service

This service is for professionals working in organisations involved in adult social care in England, for example:

  • care commissioners
  • public health officials
  • policy makers
  • care providers
  • analysts and researchers

Some datasets are restricted, so you’ll need to create an account and apply for access.

Access is subject to approval by the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC).

How to request access

Use the online service to create an account and submit your application.

DHSC will review your application and contact you with their decision, or to ask for more information.

How to sign in to the service

Once approved, you can sign in to Get adult social care data.

Online services may be slow during busy times. Check if there are any problems with this service.

Get help with this service

If you have any questions about Get adult social care data, email asc.enquiries@dhsc.gov.uk.
